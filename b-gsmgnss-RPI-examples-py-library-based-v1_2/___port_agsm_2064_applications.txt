The a-gsmII 2.105 and b-gsmgnss 2.105 shields belongs to the next generation of the a-gsm v2.064 shield.

Main differences bw. a-gsm 2.064 and a-gsmII 2.105 / b-gsmgnss 2.105 are treatead in agsm2_bgsmgnss_105_hw_control.py.

Take a look at following function definitions:
- hwControlSetup()
- resetModem()
- poweroff()
- poweron()
